# Frequently Asked Questions

Quick solutions to the questions we hear most often.

## General Questions

### What makes Kinetic Solar Solutions different from other distributors?
Unlike traditional distributors, we offer factory-direct pricing through our manufacturer partnerships, specialize in equatorial markets, provide flexible ordering for startups, and deliver comprehensive business support beyond just products. Our focus on high-insolation regions means our equipment is specifically selected for optimal performance in tropical and subtropical environments.

### Which regions do you primarily serve?
We specialize in equatorial markets across Southeast Asia (Thailand, Vietnam, Philippines, Malaysia, Indonesia), Africa (Ghana, Kenya, Nigeria, Tanzania, Uganda), and Latin America (Mexico, Colombia, Brazil, Panama, Costa Rica). However, we ship to most global locations.

### Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we provide comprehensive international shipping services to almost all countries, with particular expertise in logistics to equatorial regions. Our team handles all export documentation, shipping arrangements, and guidance on import requirements for your destination.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bank transfers/wire payments, letters of credit, and select regional payment methods. For established business customers, we also offer various credit terms and financing options based on qualification.

## Products & Ordering

### What types of solar products do you offer?
Our comprehensive product ecosystem includes solar panels (monocrystalline and polycrystalline), inverters (on-grid, off-grid, and hybrid), batteries and storage systems, mounting hardware, and balance of system components. We also offer pre-configured kits for specific applications.

### What are your minimum order quantities?
Minimum order quantities vary by product category and customer type. We offer industry-leading low MOQs for solar startups and flexible ordering options to make initial inventory investment manageable. Contact our sales team for specific minimum requirements for your situation.

### How long does delivery take?
Delivery timelines depend on product availability, order size, and destination. Typical timeframes range from 2-3 weeks for in-stock items to 6-8 weeks for large orders requiring production. Our team provides detailed delivery estimates with each quote.

### Can I order samples before placing a larger order?
Yes, we offer sample programs that allow you to evaluate our products before committing to larger inventory investments. Sample pricing and availability vary by product category.

### Do you offer custom branding/private labeling?
Yes, we provide comprehensive private labeling services for qualified partners. This includes custom branding on products, packaging, documentation, and digital assets. Minimum order requirements apply for private labeled products.

## Technical Questions

### Are your products suitable for tropical/high-temperature environments?
Absolutely. Our products are specifically selected for optimal performance in high-temperature, high-insolation environments. We choose components with appropriate temperature coefficients, enhanced heat dissipation, humidity resistance, and corrosion protection for tropical conditions.

### What certifications do your products have?
Our products carry appropriate international certifications including IEC 61215, IEC 61730, UL 1703, CE, and other relevant standards. Specific certifications vary by product category and can be provided in detail for any item you're interested in.

### Do you provide installation services?
We don't directly provide installation services, but we offer comprehensive installation guides, technical support during deployment, and can refer you to qualified local installers in many of our served regions.

### What warranty coverage comes with your products?
Our solar panels typically include 12-year product warranties and 25-year performance guarantees. Inverters generally carry 5-10 year warranties, while batteries range from 3-10 years depending on technology. Complete warranty details are provided with product specifications.

### Do you offer system design assistance?
Yes, our technical team provides system design guidance to help you configure optimal solar solutions. This includes assistance with sizing, component selection, and performance estimation.
