# Warranty & Returns

## Protection for Your Solar Investment
Comprehensive warranty coverage and straightforward return procedures.

## Warranty Commitment

Kinetic Solar Solutions stands behind every product we sell with comprehensive warranty protection. Through our direct manufacturer relationships, particularly with our primary partner Anern Solar, we provide transparent warranty coverage that gives you confidence in the long-term performance and reliability of your solar equipment.

Our warranty programs are designed to address the specific challenges of equatorial markets, with coverage terms that recognize the unique environmental conditions of tropical and subtropical regions.

## Product Warranty Coverage

### Solar Panels
Our solar modules include multi-level protection:

* **Product Warranty:** 10-12 years coverage against manufacturing defects
* **Performance Warranty:** 25-30 years linear power output guarantee
* **Coverage Details:** Protection against premature failure, excessive degradation, and manufacturing flaws
* **Performance Thresholds:** Guaranteed minimum power output percentages over time
* **Workmanship Protection:** Coverage for frame integrity, junction box issues, and connection failures

### Inverters
Comprehensive coverage for power conversion equipment:

* **Standard Coverage:** 5-10 years warranty against defects (varies by model)
* **Extended Options:** Available warranty extensions up to 20 years
* **Coverage Scope:** Protection for electronic failures, software issues, and premature wear
* **Performance Guarantee:** Assurance of conversion efficiency within specifications
* **Environmental Protection:** Coverage for properly installed units in appropriate conditions

### Batteries & Storage
Technology-specific protection for energy storage:

* **Lithium Systems:** 5-10 years warranty with cycle and capacity guarantees
* **Traditional Batteries:** 1-3 years coverage based on battery technology
* **Capacity Protection:** Guaranteed minimum capacity retention over time
* **Cycle Life Coverage:** Protected number of charge/discharge cycles
* **Component Protection:** Coverage for BMS, enclosures, and connection systems

### Mounting & Balance of System
Protection for structural and supporting components:

* **Structural Components:** 10-20 years coverage for mounting hardware
* **Electrical Components:** 1-5 years warranty for balance of system items
* **Corrosion Protection:** Coverage against premature environmental degradation
* **Load Rating Assurance:** Guaranteed performance under specified wind/snow loads
* **Material Integrity:** Protection against manufacturing defects and material failure

## Warranty Process

Our streamlined warranty service includes:

1. **Claim Initiation:** Simple online or email-based claim submission
2. **Documentation:** Clear guidance on required evidence and information
3. **Evaluation:** Prompt assessment of warranty claim validity
4. **Resolution Options:** Repair, replacement, or compensation based on claim type
5. **Shipping Coordination:** Managed logistics for warranty replacements
6. **Documentation:** Complete records of warranty service for your protection

## Return Policy

For non-warranty returns of unused equipment:

* **Return Window:** 30-day return period for unused, undamaged products
* **Condition Requirements:** Original packaging and unaltered product condition
* **Documentation:** Return authorization process with tracking
* **Restocking Fees:** 15-25% fee may apply depending on product category
* **Shipping Responsibility:** Return shipping costs borne by customer
* **Exclusions:** Custom-ordered and private-labeled products are non-returnable

## Regional Warranty Support

We provide localized warranty service through:

* **Regional Service Centers:** Technical support in key market hubs
* **Partner Network:** Qualified service providers in major markets
* **Remote Diagnostics:** Technical evaluation without physical inspection
* **International Shipping:** Warranty replacement to any service location
* **Documentation Support:** Assistance with regional certification requirements

## Warranty Transfers

Our flexible warranty terms include:

* **Business Transfers:** Continued coverage when products are sold between businesses
* **End-User Transfers:** Warranty protection for secondary consumers
* **Documentation Process:** Simple warranty transfer documentation
* **Verification System:** Validation of warranty eligibility and remaining coverage
* **Continuous Protection:** Uninterrupted coverage through ownership changes
